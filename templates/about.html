About the Wildfire Prediction Application

This app was built to help visualize areas at risk for a wildfire in the United States.

This project uses historic monthly temperature, precipitation, and humidity data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) and historic wildfire data from the United States Forest Service. A logistic regression model was fit on these data and used to predict likelihood (predicited probiablity) of a wildfire occuring for a given month using current monthly data from NOAA.

Current wildfires have been overlayed on top of the risk map.
